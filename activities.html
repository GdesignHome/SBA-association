Dancing and theater has transformed the lives of he group :

Many who were discourage about going to school have once again decided to give it a shot through the promotion of the importance of education, this has had impact mostly on two of our members who are girls. Dancing has created self confidence in group members. Two of our dancers are known in our local market for dancing at the square of the market. This too was done by two girls from the group, that alone created awareness about the deaf community in the market. Silence Breakers Academy has also become a safe Space for the deaf community as most of them share their personal experiences and challenges integrating with the community. We use theater to advocate for their rights and change the perspective that Deafs are beggers. Silence Breakers Academy has build leadership skills in group members. Through sharing of tasks and given each member an opportunity or acting to share their thoughts Infront of the rest of the crew regarding a particular topic.

Outreach and Impact

We believe in giving back to our community and making a positive impact. Through our outreach programs, we aim to inspire and empower individuals with hearing impairments, spreading the joy of dance and theater far and wide.

Our dance group is participating in World Disability Day, an annual event aimed at raising awareness about disability rights and promoting inclusivity. We created a powerful video to celebrate the talents and resilience of individuals with disabilities. The video aims to inspire conversations about inclusivity, accessibility, and equal opportunities for all. This Year's Theme was "UNITED IN ACTION TO RESCUE AND ACHIEVE THE SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS OF, WITH AND BY PERSON WITH DISABILITIES". The group's performance showcases the power of dance to transcend boundaries and communicate emotions without words. We hope to spread the message of inclusivity and diversity in the world.
